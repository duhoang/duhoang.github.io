MapD Tweet Map

I helped MapD design and build their Tweet Map data visualization using D3.js and MapBox GL.

They needed a beautiful demo to impress investors, and to that end, I designed a bold, modern, and highly interactive tweet map that was partly inspired by traditional Japanese screen prints.

Data Visualization Dashboard

I also redesigned MapD data visualization dashboard platform.

The key here is to allow the user to quickly and intuatively build powerful and attractive data visualization reports. With the emphasis being on data exploration over window dressing, and simplicity over customization.

Tweet Map Interactions

My philosophy is that interactive data visualization should be more than a collection of static views. And with the thoughtful use of animation and transition, my aim is to bring it to life.
