MapD Tweet Map

MapD needed a beautiful data visualization demo to impress potential investors and customers. To that end I designed and build a bold, modern, highly interactive tweet map using D3.js and MapBoxGL.

The visual design of the map is inspired by the colors and lines of Japanese woodblock paintings.

Data Visualization Dashboard

In the competitive field of data visualization dashboards, MapD needed innovate design to differentiate itself from the rest of the pack.

The key is to leapfrog the current industry standard, which is mired in decades of corporate application design, with its obtuse visual language and steep learning curve.

So I created new design paradigms with the aim of bringing clarity and simplicity to the complex world of big data exploration.

Tweet Map Interactions

My philosophy is that interactive data visualization should be more than a collection of static views. And with the thoughtful use of animation and transitions, my goal is to bring it to life.
