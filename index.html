AdFin Analytics Platform
1 2

I joined AdFin shortly after the launch of their MVP. My challenge was to transform a rather dry and overwhelmingly technical analytics platform into something that is visually attractive and user friendly.

To do this I use bold typography and colors that clearly convey the hierachy of the information, and drastically simplified the graphs and charts so that the data can be easier to digest.

Working with AdFin allowed me to explore the craft of data visualization. It also introduced me to Object-Oriented Javascript programming, D3.js data visualization framework, and Facebook's own javascript application framework, React.js.

AdFin Responsive Website
1 2 3

AdFin historically have had trouble communicating their two divergent products and value propositions to potential clients. So for the landing page I split the screen into halves to visually express the separation between their B.I. tool, and market reports.

The feedback of the redesign was overwhelmingly positive. One client said that our site was "one of the best in the industry, and clearly articulate what AdFin does."

On the website I used beautiful data visualizations as a motif to emphasize our strength and authority in that area, and to build confidence in our data visualization products.

The website is also responsive and attractive on any device. And that allows us to capture leads from every portal.
