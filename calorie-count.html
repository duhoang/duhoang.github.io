Calorie Count, Food & Fitness Social Network

I was recruited to transform Calorie Count from a dated SEO driven website into a modern, design-focused, web and mobile social network.

I introduced a social feed and game mechanics to their food & activity tracking apps, added food photo sharing, build the iPad app and mobile site from scratch, streamlined the UX, and redesigned all of the interfaces.

iOS, Android & Mobile Web Apps
1 2 3 4

The bulk of my work at Calorie Count was in transitioning the product from desktop to mobile.

I carefully studied the design patterns and asthetics of each platform, and designed a family of apps that is cohesive in style but at the same time look and feel native to each device.

I've also taught myself enough Objective-C do the layout and animation on the iPhone app, and take the design burden off of our iOS engineers.

I designed and built an HTML5 mobile framework for our mobile web app. It is AJAX and persistent, and feels like a native app, with smooth transitions and animations between menus and screens.
